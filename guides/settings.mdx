## Connection

- **Adapter IP**: Default `192.168.4.1` for WiFi adapters
- **Adapter Port**: Default `6801`
- **Connect / Disconnect**: Toggle connection from Settings or the connection status in the header

---

## Units

- **Temperature**: °C or °F (affects Live tab gauges)
- **Pressure**: PSI, BAR, or kPa (if applicable)

---

## Supported Adapters

- MHD WiFi (HSFZ over TCP)
- ENET cable (HSFZ over TCP)

---

## Supported Vehicles

Apex supports BMW vehicles with:

- N54, N55, S55 (MEVD17.2)
- B58 Gen1, B58 Gen2 (MG1)
- S58 (MD1)
- B48 Gen2, S63, N13

The full vehicle list (chassis codes, model names, production years) is available in the app under **Settings** → **Hardware** → **Supported Vehicles**.

---

## Account & About

- **Account**: Device info, connection status, export logs, reset preferences
- **About**: App version, build, platform, license
