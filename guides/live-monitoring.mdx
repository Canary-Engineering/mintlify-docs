## Gauges

The **Live** tab displays real-time ECU data:

- **Hero gauges**: Boost, RPM, AFR (air-fuel ratio)
- **Secondary**: IAT, Coolant, EGT, WGDC, Timing, Throttle
- **Per-cylinder timing**: Expandable bars for each cylinder
- **Extended data**: Boost Target, Voltage, Fuel Pressure, Oil Temp/Pressure, Trans Temp, Speed, Knock Corr., Torque Lim.

---

## Units

Toggle between °C/°F for temperature in **Settings** or via the Live tab controls.

---

## Data Logging

1. Open the **Live** tab
2. Tap **LOG** to start recording
3. Tap **STOP** to end the session
4. Logged sessions appear in the **Saved Logs** list

## Channel Presets

Default channels include boost, RPM, AFR, IAT, coolant, timing. Custom channel presets can be imported for tuner-specific logging configurations. Channel availability depends on your ECU and ROM.
