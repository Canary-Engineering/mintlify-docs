## Overview

Apex supports BMW vehicles with N54, N55, S55, B58, S58, and B48 Gen2 engines. The vehicle database maps chassis codes, model names, and production years to engine families.

## Chassis Generations

- **E-Series**: E82, E9x, E60 (E9x 335i)
- **F-Series**: F2x, F3x, F8x (M140i, M240i, 340i, 440i, M235i, M3, M4)
- **G-Series**: G01, G2x, G29, G80, G82, A90 (X3 M40i, M340i, Z4 M40i, Supra, M3, M4)

## App Access

The full vehicle list is available in the app under **Settings** → **Hardware** → **Supported Vehicles**.
